md 1.2 — write a whole book, not just a page.

• Writer mode: open a folder as a book — chapters, articles, private notes that never print. Compile it to one PDF, or export it as EPUB.
• Split now scrolls as one: move either pane, the other follows.
• PDFs are real A4 pages, line-aware, with \newpage where you want it. White paper, dark ink.
• Live word and character count. Math and diagrams. Images.
• Legacy Cyrillic files open as text, not mojibake.
