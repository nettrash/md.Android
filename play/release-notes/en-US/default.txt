md 1.2 — PDFs done right, and writer tools.

• Share or export the rendered document as a PDF: continuous content-tall pages, no lines cut — with \newpage giving you your own page breaks.
• Table of contents: jump to any heading; autosave keeps the file current as you type.
• Writer mode: open a folder as a book — chapters, articles, private <!-- note: --> comments that never print.
• Still fully offline: no network, no accounts, zero permissions (not even INTERNET).
