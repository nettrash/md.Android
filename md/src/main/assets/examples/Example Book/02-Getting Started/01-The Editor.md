# The Editor

md keeps writing simple: one document, three ways to view it.

- **Edit** shows the raw Markdown — best for focused typing.
- **Preview** shows the rendered page — best for reading.
- **Split** shows both, and the preview follows as you type.

Choose the mode that fits the moment; switching is instant and never
changes the document itself.

## Finding your way

Long document? Open the **Contents** menu — it lists every heading and
jumps straight to it. Keep your headings meaningful and navigation comes
for free.

> Tip: while drafting, leave yourself a private `<!-- note: ... -->`
> comment. It shows up in the **Notes** menu but never in the preview
> or an export.
